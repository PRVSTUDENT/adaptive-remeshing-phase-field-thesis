# Thesis Evidence Freeze

Stage E freezes the current thesis claims and evidence package for final review.

## Included

- scoped Stage C accuracy/cost evidence;
- Stage D analytical transfer, ingestion, continuation, repeatability,
  nonmatching transfer, R4 release hold, fixed-active-set invalidation, and
  offline correction evidence;
- D3D-A1H0 execution limitation;
- final figures, tables, claim matrix, tolerance policy, ABAQUSER closure,
  recommendations, and reproducibility manifest.

## Excluded claims

- post-peak or crack-path equivalence for Stage C;
- an accepted D3D-A1 mechanical restart;
- D3E or post-peak state-transfer continuation;
- online adaptive remeshing;
- ABAQUSER agreement;
- universal supervisor approval of provisional tolerances.

## Execution boundary

No Abaqus solver, datacheck, phase-release, continuation, D3E, or R3 job is
authorized. A future mechanical-restart investigation requires a new,
independent decision after thesis review.
