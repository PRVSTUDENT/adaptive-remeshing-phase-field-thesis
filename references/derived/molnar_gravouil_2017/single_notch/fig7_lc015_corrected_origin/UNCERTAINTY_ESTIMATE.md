# Digitization Uncertainty Estimate

Classification: `approximate_digitized_publication_reference`

Approximate coordinate uncertainty:

- displacement: about `+/- 0.00005 mm`
- force: about `+/- 0.02 kN`

Uncertainty is larger near the peak and post-peak drop where published marker
outlines and overlapping curves are dense. These coordinates are a
publication-image digitization, not exact author numerical data.
